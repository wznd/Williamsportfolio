About Me


Hello! I am a Product Designer based in New York City with a background in Finance. I am well-versed in the client-facing environment, and also taught myself the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, JavaScript in college.
‍
Intuitive design is my biggest inspiration and ideal. To me, intuitive design always puts the user's needs in the forefront, while also being efficient and effortless. In my five years in the financial field, I have developed an analytical approach to problem-solving which in turn, helped me focus on the bigger picture when diving into user research. 
‍
My goal is to hone these skills as a designer in an emerging industry, particularly with Accessibility and Tech Addiction.
