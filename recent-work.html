Home Monitoring Dashboard

A tablet based screen of a home monitoring dashboard app, featuring smart home control modules and IOT collected data reporting. Compatible with most smart home technologies including smart speakers, security cameras, doorbells, and smart lights. Also included were elements of a social media platform inclusive to all members of the household. This would allow for more efficient allocations of responsibilities and may also include smart home personalization based on the members habits and schedule.

Aesthetically, it draws influence from both skeuomorphic and flat design. I decided to include elements of skeuomorphism on the interactive portions of the dashboard such as the thermostat dial or the toggle buttons. This was to draw attention to the users on what could be interacted with as well as to create a sense of familiarity with real life devices.
